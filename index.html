Welcome to Little Spoon! My name is Lucy, and I'm the founder of Little Spoon, a creative studio specialising in marketing, design, and printing. Little Spoon was founded after I moved to Ohakune and decided to put my passion and qualifications in design and marketing to use. In my spare time I enjoy baking, photography, cat-cuddling, and having a laugh.
